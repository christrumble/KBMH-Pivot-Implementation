Pivot Interiors Overview 
Founded in 1973, Pivot Interiors, Inc. has grown to become one of the largest Certified MillerKnoll Dealers, offering end-to-end solutions for various sectors including Tech, Healthcare, Government, and Commercial12. The company operates from multiple locations across California, with its headquarters in Santa Clara and additional offices in Fremont, San Francisco, Costa Mesa, Los Angeles, and La Mirada34.
As a Platinum Level member of the MillerKnoll Certified Dealer Network, Pivot demonstrates a strong commitment to consistently outperforming customer expectations5. The company's half-century of experience has allowed it to adapt to changing workplace trends and economic cycles, positioning it as a leader in workplace design and furniture solutions6.
