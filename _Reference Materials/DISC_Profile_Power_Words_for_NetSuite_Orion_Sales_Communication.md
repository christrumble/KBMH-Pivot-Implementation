DISC Profile Power Words for NetSuite/Orion Sales Communication

## Overview and Objective

This document serves as a comprehensive guide for tailoring our sales and implementation communication strategies when promoting NetSuite/Orion to furniture dealers. Its primary objective is to help our sales team effectively communicate with decision-makers by aligning our language and approach with their DISC (Dominant, Influential, Steady, Conscientious) personality profiles.

By using this guide, we aim to:
1. Identify the DISC profiles of key decision-makers at prospective client companies.
2. Customize our communication style, word choice, and presentation approach to resonate with each decision-maker's preferences and priorities.
3. Emphasize the aspects of NetSuite/Orion that are most likely to appeal to each DISC profile type.
4. Increase our effectiveness in addressing client concerns and highlighting the value of our solution.
5. Ultimately improve our success rate in closing deals and implementing NetSuite/Orion for furniture dealers.

This document should be used throughout the sales process, from initial contact to final negotiations and implementation discussions. It is a living document that can be updated as we gain more insights and refine our approach based on real-world interactions with furniture dealer decision-makers.

## Using This Guide for NetSuite/Orion Sales

1. Identify the primary DISC profile of your decision-maker through conversation analysis and behavior observation.
2. When discussing NetSuite/Orion, incorporate power words that align with their profile to emphasize the aspects of the system that will resonate most with them.
3. Adjust your presentation style to match their preferences:
   - D: Focus on ROI and competitive advantages
   - I: Highlight user-friendly features and success stories
   - S: Emphasize reliability and smooth implementation process
   - C: Provide detailed information on accuracy and process improvements
4. Tailor your demo approach based on their DISC profile:
   - D: Show high-level dashboards and quick ROI calculations
   - I: Demonstrate collaborative features and customizable interfaces
   - S: Walk through the implementation process and support options
   - C: Deep dive into reporting capabilities and data accuracy features
5. Remember that individuals may have traits from multiple profiles. In such cases, blend approaches as appropriate.
6. Continuously refine your approach based on the decision-maker's responses and feedback throughout the sales process.

By aligning our NetSuite/Orion sales approach with each decision-maker's DISC profile, we can more effectively communicate the value of our solution, address their specific concerns, and increase our chances of closing deals successfully.
